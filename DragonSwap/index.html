Dragon Swap
DSC offers services for aggregating information over exchange networks. The heart of the protocol is the DSC smart contract, which in turn validates the verified transaction. As a result, the participant's funds cannot be lost in the event of interaction with an unsafe source of liquidity. DSC provides security for the protocol and can be used in various information services.
Safe Liquidity Protocol
SLP offers services for aggregating information over exchange networks. The heart of the protocol is the SLP smart contract, which in turn validates the verified transaction.

As a result, the participant's funds cannot be lost in the event of interaction with an unsafe source of liquidity. SLP provides security for the protocol and can be used in various information services.
Farming
Collect additional rewards in SLP tokens for participating in farming programs. To receive passive income of up to 100% per annum from your cryptoassets, you just need to provide liquidity to the SLP pools. Liquidity providers collect rewards for assets locked in pools and rewards for additional profitability in SLP tokens.
